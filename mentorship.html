🎓 After 10th - Stream Selection Guidance

Choose the right path for your future career

Science Stream (PCM/PCB)

  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM)

    Best for: Engineering, Architecture, Computer Science, Data Science, Robotics, Aerospace

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB)

    Best for: Medicine (MBBS), Dentistry (BDS), Pharmacy, Biotechnology, Nursing, Veterinary

  • PCM + Biology (PCMB)

    Keeps both Engineering and Medical options open. Ideal if you're undecided.

Commerce Stream

  • Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics

    Best for: CA, CS, CMA, B.Com, BBA, MBA, Banking, Finance, Entrepreneurship

  • With Mathematics

    Opens doors to Economics Honors, Statistics, Actuarial Science

Arts/Humanities Stream

  • History, Political Science, Economics, Psychology

    Best for: Law, Journalism, Mass Communication, Psychology, Social Work, Civil Services

  • Creative Arts

    Fine Arts, Design, Fashion, Photography, Film Making, Animation
